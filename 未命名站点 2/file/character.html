Maya(processing)

 Maya is a young woman in her mid-20s with a slender, athletic build honed by her life in the harsh city. She has long, dark hair that she usually ties back in a practical ponytail or braid, and her eyes are a deep, striking green that can be both captivating and intense. Her skin is tanned, and her face has a natural beauty that she often downplays, preferring to focus on her skills and abilities. Maya dresses in functional clothing that allow her to move easily through the city undetected. Her outfit typically consists of a dark, fitted jacket, cargo pants, sturdy boots, gloves, and a scarf to protect her from the polluted environment.

Jackson(processing)

Jackson is an African American man in his late 20s with a strong, muscular build that commands attention. He has closely cropped black hair and intense, dark brown eyes that convey confidence and determination. His face is chiselled, with high cheekbones and a square jawline that give him a striking appearance. Jackson's wardrobe reflects his role as the charismatic leader of The Uprising. He typically wears tactical clothing, including a form-fitting black jacket, combat pants, and durable boots. He also sports fingerless gloves and various lightweight armour for added protection during missions.

Leo(processing)

Leo is a brilliant scientist in his early 30s, with a tall, slender build that makes him stand out in a crowd. His brown hair is slightly unkempt, a testament to his long hours working on his research and inventions. He has warm, hazel eyes that shine with curiosity and intelligence. His face is youthful, with a touch of stubble that he rarely bothers to shave. Leo's attire is practical and comfortable, consisting of a lab coat or a worn-out sweater, a simple button-up shirt, and jeans or khakis. He always carries a pair of safety goggles and gloves for when he's working in his lab, and his pockets are often filled with various tools and gadgets.
