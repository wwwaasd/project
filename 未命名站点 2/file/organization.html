Genesis Corp(processing)

'Genesis Corp' is a mysterious organization that sprang up with astonishing speed after the 'Cataclysm'. It showed great power and control of the rich resources to allow it to control the 'New Star City' in the first place. The rapid formation of the organization has led many to suspect that it was formed before the 'Cataclysm' and had some foresight. Whatever, Genesis is undoubtedly one of the most powerful human organizations in 'New Era'.
