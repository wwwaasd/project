You decided to visit communities around your house because you had to walk, and you didn’t want to make yourself tired. When you were walking along the road in the nearby community, you suddenly heard a burst of noise, and then following with roar of people. That was the first time that you headed such noise, and you didn’t know what that noise was. You noticed that many well-dressed people were running in terror like they were escaping from something. You didn’t know what happened, but you started to run with them. Finally, when you started to feel tired, you were caught by servants looking for you. You were brough home and for the first time you were punished by you parents. They forced you to kneel on the floor and lashed you on your back. You then never left your community later in your childhood.
