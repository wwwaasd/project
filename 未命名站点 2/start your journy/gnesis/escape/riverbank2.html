You decided to visit the riverbank far away from your home even though you needed to walk for a long time. You believed that you could make it. You then walked for hours, the Sun set down and the Moon went up. You were still far away from the riverbank. You felt exhausted. Suddenly, you were stopped by a man and a woman holding a baby. They were all dirty with greasy clothes and you smelt the stink from their bodies. You thought they would hurt you, so you stepped back and were ready to run away from them. But out of your expectation, they just begged for some food and water. However, you were terrified and ran away. You were caught by servants looking for you when you were running.
